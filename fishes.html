There are ALOT of Fishes to Choose From

There a lot of fishes to actually pick as a starter. However, I will be listing the best starter fishes. These fishes have the best personality and
The first one being The Common Molly.

The Common Molly

Mollies are a great addition to you tanks. They are playful, eats a lot, and groups up as well. They are a schooling fish so keeping 3-4 of them in your tank will make them a happy fish and make your aquarium look active. They are a very tough fish. Plus they come in different colors and mixes as well.

Glofish Danios

Danios are a very fast schooling fish. They zoom around the tank in groups so keep 4-6 of them in your tank. They are very colorful and they glow then shined by a light source. They are very resilient and fun to watch in your aquarium.

Balloon Molly

Balloon Mollies are another type of Molly, these molly have a bloated belly and are more chunkier than the common molly. They are also a schooling fish, and are very funny to watch due to how chunky they are.

Betta fish or Siamese Fighthing Fish

Betta Fishes are very colorful and attractive fishes with huge tails. BE WARNED! Betta Fishes are very territorial. Unless you have a huge tank with a lot of hiding spaces, do not keep Betta Fishes with other Fishes. Betta Fishes can only be kept together if they are females. Male Bettas will Fight each other and could lead to death. Females on the other hand, could live with each other.

Platy or Sun Fish

Platy's are another small fish that are easy to care for. They are small and also like to school. They are like molly's but smaller. These should also be together with atleast 3-5 of their own kind.

That is all for Starter Fishes!
